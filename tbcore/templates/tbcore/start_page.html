How to use the tool box

This tool box walks you through 8 building blocks of a sound online course. Per building block, different teaching tools and technologies are suggested to meet the minimum requirements for online courses. The details of each teaching tool help you to estimate the effort you have to make, and you can take notes which will be saved to a personalized checklist which you can use for course preparation.

Further general teaching tips, news and updates about technology supported by VirtUOS are published on the Digitale Lehre Portal. Additionally, all (topic-related) walk-in hours and meetings about trending topics from the didactical colloquium "Lehrkolleg" are announced there.
